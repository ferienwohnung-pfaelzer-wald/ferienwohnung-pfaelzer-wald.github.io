The accomodation

Our house is located in Langensohl in the beautiful Palatinate Forest, not far from Kaiserslautern. Whether hiking, cycling or simply relaxing – you are warmly welcome.

We also live in the house ourselves, but you have a separate apartment with its own entrance, kitchen, bathroom and living room. There is also a garden with a grill and fire pit. In the garden you will occasionally meet us.
