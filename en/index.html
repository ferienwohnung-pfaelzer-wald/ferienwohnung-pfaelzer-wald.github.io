Family-friendly accommodation in the tranquil Palatinate Forest

The accomodation

Our house is located in Langensohl within the nice Pfälzer Wald (Palatinate Forest) not far from Kaiserslautern (K-Town). Whether you want to go hiking, biking, or just relax, you are very more than welcome here.

We live in the house ourselves, but you have your own separate apartment with your own entrance, kitchen, bathroom and living room. There is also a garden with barbecue and fireplace. In the garden you will also meet us from time to time.

The kitchen has dishwasher, fridge/freezer, stove, oven, coffee machine, toaster, kettle.

In the bathroom there is both a shower and a bathtub.

There is a large bedroom with double bed (140x200) and a small bedroom with single bed. In the living room there is a couch that can be easily converted for sleeping.

In total there is room for five people. In the large bedroom there is a double bed for two people. In the small bedroom there is a single bed. In the living room on the sofa bed can sleep another two persons.

There is a washing machine in the kitchen for longer stays.

In the living room there is a TV.

Of course there is also Wi-Fi available.
