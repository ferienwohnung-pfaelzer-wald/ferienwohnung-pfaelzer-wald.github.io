The accomodation

Our house is located in Langensohl within the nice PfÃ¤lzer Wald (Palatinate Forest) not far from Kaiserslautern (K-Town). Whether you want to go hiking, biking, or just relax, you are very more than welcome here.

We live in the house ourselves, but you have your own separate apartment with your own entrance, kitchen, bathroom and living room. There is also a garden with barbecue and fireplace. In the garden you will also meet us from time to time.

The kitchen has dishwasher, fridge/freezer, stove, oven, coffee machine (bring your own Dolce Gusto capsules), toaster, kettle.

In the bathroom there is both a shower and a bathtub.

In total there is room for four people. In the large bedroom there is a double bed for two people. In the small bedroom there is a single bed. In the living room on the sofa bed can sleep another person.

On the TV in the living room, you can watch Netflix.

Of course there is also wifi available.
